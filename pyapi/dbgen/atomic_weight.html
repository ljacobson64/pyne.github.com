Atomic Weights – pyne.dbgen.atomic_weight¶

This module adds high-fidelity atomic weights and natural abundance data to nuc_data.h5.

All functionality may be found in the atomic_weight module:

from pyne.dbgen import atomic_weight

Atomic Weights API¶

This module provides a way to grab and store raw data for atomic weights.

pyne.dbgen.atomic_weight.copy_atomic_mass_adjustment(build_dir='')[source]¶

Copies the atomic mass evaluation originally from the Atomic Mass Data Center. These are courtesy of Georges Audi and Wang Meng via a private communication, November 2012.
